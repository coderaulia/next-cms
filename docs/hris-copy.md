FEATURE BREAKDOWN
Section Header:
Everything Your HR Team Needs to Run a Fair, Consistent, and Documented Appraisal Process

Feature 1: Competency Assessments
Define the competency frameworks that matter for your organisation. Score employees consistently against configurable criteria — not guesswork, not gut feeling. Every assessment is recorded, traceable, and reportable.

Feature 2: KPI Governance
Set targets. Track progress. Run approvals. KPI definitions flow from HR down to managers and employees with a built-in workflow — so everyone knows what's expected, and results are documented when review season arrives.

Feature 3: Probation & PIP Workflows
Never miss a probation deadline again. Vanaila tracks every new hire's probation period, flags upcoming milestones, and keeps a full audit trail of performance improvement plans. HR stays in control. Managers stay informed.

Feature 4: HR Documents — Generated in Seconds
From warning letters to employment contracts, generate legally formatted HR documents directly from the platform. Templates are editable, signer-ready, and export as clean A4 PDFs. No more copying and pasting into Word at 5pm on a Friday.

Feature 5: Dashboard & Reporting
Aggregated performance views for directors and HR leadership. Export KPI and probation reports without digging through spreadsheets. Real data, on demand.

Feature 6: Role-Aware Access, Always
Employees. Managers. HR. Directors. Every user sees exactly what they should — nothing more, nothing less. Built-in role separation means no accidental data exposure and no permission headaches.
